  Angles
  • Sum of the Interior or internal angle of an n-gon = (n - 2) x 180
  • Measure of each interior or internal angle of a regular polygon = (n – 2) x 180
  • Sum of the exterior angles of n-gon is = 360 degree
  • The measure of each exterior angle of a regular polygon is = 360/n
  • Sum of the external angles of the polygon = (n + 2) x 180
  Triangles
  • Pythagorean theorm
  • By Pythagorean theorem, for a right angle triangle, z2= x2+ y2
  • If z2> x2+ y2 , the angle formed is an obtuse angle.
  • If z2< x2+ y2 , the angle formed is an acute angle.
  Special cases for triangles
  • The ratio of the sides of 45-45-90 right angled triangle is 1 : 1 : √2
  • The ratio of the sides of 30-60-90 right angled triangle is 1 : √3 : 2
